About Us

Nextrive Technologies is a future-focused tech company based in Nigeria, committed to delivering impactful technology solutions. Founded by industry professionals with a strong background in software engineering and digital strategy, we offer scalable solutions tailored to businesses, institutions, and individuals.

We are passionate about bridging the digital divide and helping organizations thrive in the digital era through smart applications, cybersecurity services, and impactful training programs. Our core team consists of certified experts driven by a mission to transform Africa’s digital ecosystem through innovation and quality service delivery.

Our Services

  • Custom Software Development: Web, mobile, and desktop applications tailored for performance and security.
  • Cybersecurity Solutions: Full-stack security auditing, training, and threat mitigation services.
  • Biometric Solutions: Advanced attendance, access control, and verification systems using fingerprint/face ID.
  • CBT & School Portals: Complete online systems for exams, results, student management, and staff tracking.
  • Digital Skills Training: Practical IT training programs in web development, data analysis, and more.
  • Business Website Design: Elegant, mobile-friendly, and SEO-optimized websites for companies and organizations.
  • POS & Inventory Systems: Robust systems for small businesses and large-scale retail operations.
  • Cloud Deployment & IT Consulting: Helping businesses scale with cloud migration, IT support, and infrastructure planning.
